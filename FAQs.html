FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS



What is the cost?

The customization price varies according to the design. It depends on the complexity of design, colors, size, and the time it takes to do.

Do they ship?

We make express international shipments, Parcels and delivery time may vary depending on availability. National shipments are free.

How long does a customization take?

The process takes about a week, however it can be extended. It depends on the orders we have before yours, the design of your order, and the availability of Jeans in the official stores. We keep you informed during the time and progress process and send a photo once it is ready.

Can I wash it? Does the paint fall off?

We use textile paint especially for use in top-quality fabric, when finished we seal with heat to adhere the paint with the fabric. You can wash and use them normally.
